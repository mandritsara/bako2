During your me in Mandritsara, you might have the opportunity of
vising villages around the region. This could be a day trip on foot/
motorbike, or a few days with the community team by helicopter.
It is helpful to equip yourself with some basic vocabulary, including a few
adjecves that might come in handy.

How was your visit to the village?
